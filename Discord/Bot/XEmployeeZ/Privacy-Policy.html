Privacy Policy for XplodeZ GamerZ Discord Bot

Effective Date: October 25, 2025

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the XplodeZ GamerZ Discord Bot ("Bot") handles information. We are committed to transparency and minimal data collection, in line with user trust and privacy best practices. By using the Bot, you consent to this policy.

1. Data Collection Practices

The Bot collects no data from Discord users or servers.

It collects information from YouTube videos (with fallback to manual input) to send announcements to a specific channel in the XplodeZ GamerZ Discord server.

Data is collected solely for the purpose of posting updates on latest videos.

Collection occurs via YouTube APIs or manual entry, as configured by the user.

2. Data Usage and Sharing

Collected data (e.g., video links or details) is used only to generate and send announcements within the designated Discord channel.

Data is not shared with third parties, stored long-term, or used for any other purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose user data.

3. User Rights

Users have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any data associated with their use of the Bot.

For assistance with privacy inquiries, contact: useanything110@gmail.com.

Users can opt out by stopping use of the Bot or leaving the Discord server.

4. Third-Party Integrations

The Bot integrates with YouTube. For details on YouTube's privacy practices, please refer to their official privacy policy: YouTube Privacy Policy.

Other services like Discord and Telegram may be used; users should review their respective policies for age requirements and data handling.

5. Children's Privacy

The Bot does not collect personal data from users.

Anyone can use the Bot, but Discord and other external services' age requirements (e.g., minimum age for accounts) still apply. We do not target or collect data from children under applicable laws.

6. Updates and Contact Info

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be notified via our Discord server, Telegram channel, WhatsApp channel, and, if required, in our YouTube videos.

For questions or concerns, contact:

  • General Help: moleculartechnology1@gmail.com
  • Assistance or Support: useanything110@gmail.com

We strive to protect your privacy. If you have feedback, let us know!
